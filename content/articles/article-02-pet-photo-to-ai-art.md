### Step 1: Choose Your Best Photo

The foundation of a great AI pet portrait is a good source photo. Here's what works best:

**Photo Quality Checklist:**

| Criteria | Ideal | Avoid |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Resolution | 1080px minimum on shortest side | Blurry or pixelated images |
| Lighting | Natural daylight or soft indoor lighting | Harsh shadows or overexposure |
| Background | Simple, uncluttered | Busy backgrounds that compete with your pet |
| Pose | Clear view of face and distinctive features | Extreme angles or partially obscured faces |
| Expression | Natural, characteristic expression | Mid-blink or awkward mouth positions |

**Pro tip:** The AI needs to see what makes your pet *your pet*. That distinctive white patch on your dog's chest? The unique pattern of your cat's whiskers? Make sure those details are visible in your source photo.

**Dogs:** Capture them at eye level for the most engaging portraits. The AI excels at rendering expressive eyes and distinctive coat textures.

**Rabbits and Small Pets:** Get close enough to capture facial details. The Embroidery Art style creates surprisingly beautiful results with these subjects.
